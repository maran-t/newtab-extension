Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines the practices regarding the handling of user data for the New Tab Chrome extension. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

No Data Collection

The New Tab Chrome extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or any form of user information. The extension operates entirely locally on your browser and does not interact with any external servers or services.

No Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services or APIs that would result in data collection, sharing, or transmission. All functionality is performed locally within your browser.

User Consent and Permissions

The extension may request certain permissions (such as access to specific websites or browser tabs), but these are only required for the extension to function as intended. None of these permissions involve the collection or sharing of personal data.
